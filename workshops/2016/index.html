Workshop at ADHO DH 2016, Kraków

During the DH2016 conference in Krakow, the AVinDH SIG organised a workshop with the theme ‘Audiovisual Data and Digital Scholarship: Towards Multimodal Literacy’. The workshop took place Tuesday 12 July in the MAM / Aula Mala / Small Auditorium of the main Conference Venue, the Auditorium Maximum, ul. Krupnicza 33. 

We are currently gathering all presentations and are writing a report on the workshop. In the meantime you can find all relevant documents below or read this wonderful report on the workshop by Liliane Melgers.

Program
